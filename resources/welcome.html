This is a small prototype to demonstrate a simple question and answer capture.

A questionnaire is developed in a data format called EDN (pron: Eden) which is shown below

This questionnaire can be filled out by clicking on the Questionnaire in the menu above

Already filled out questionnaires are visible via the Submissions menu item. The submitted responses are also in EDN - they are basically the questionnaire itself with answers added.

It is possible to post-process these submissions to provide insights

This prototype is extremely basic, but provides a discussion point for potential directions
